Overview

This week is your Quiz. To prepare for the quiz you should have completed all the C++ tutorials, completed the review exercises from last week and reviewed the lecture material up to last Friday's lecture. The quiz is going to be a combination of questions involving design, writing and interpreting C++, and writing and interpreting UML. Although we don't teach UML directly in this course, it is assumed that you understand the underlying concepts which were taught in the prerequisite course INFO2110.

Quiz

The quiz is a paper based quiz to give you practice with writing C++ using pen and paper, with the aim of helping you prepare for the exam.
