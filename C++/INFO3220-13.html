Overview

This week you will present your final demo, where after you may choose to stick around and attempt the sample exam.

Sample Exam

There is a sample exam available on eLearning. The sample exam is designed to be the same length and the same number of questions and parts as the final exam. It also follows the same format.

Sample Exam Solutions

The solutions for the sample exam will not be released. There are ways in which the solutions can be accessed however. For Questions 1 through 3 you may ask and post solutions on piazza and we will aim to guide you until you come up with the correct answers.

Question 4 and Question 5 will be discussed in detail in the lecture on Friday were we will hopefully construct a complete solution.

I would strongly suggest looking at the sample exam before the lecture this week so that you can ask questions now. It allows you to find out what you do know and what you need to study.

Review the tutorials

Once you have completed the sample exam make sure that you review each tutorial you have completed this semester. All tutorials offer useful hints which will assist in your final exam.
